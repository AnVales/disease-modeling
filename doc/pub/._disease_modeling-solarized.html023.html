What is the effect of an intensive vaccination campaign?

10 times more intense vaccination for 10 days, 6 days after outbreak: $$ \begin{equation*} p(t) = \left\lbrace\begin{array}{ll} 0.05,& 6\leq t\leq 15,\\ 0,& \hbox{otherwise} \end{array}\right.\end{equation*} $$

Implementation: Let \( p^n \) be an array as \( V^n \). Set \( p^n=0.05 \) for \( n=6\cdot 24/0.1,\ldots, 15\cdot 24/0.1 \) (\( \mbox{days}\cdot 24 \mbox{h per day}\Delta t \)).
